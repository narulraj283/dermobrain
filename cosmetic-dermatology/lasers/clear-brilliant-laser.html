Clear+Brilliant Laser: Gentle Fractional Rejuvenation

Written by DermoBrain Medical Team Medically Reviewed

Overview of Clear+Brilliant Technology

Clear+Brilliant, manufactured by Solta Medical, represents the gentlest option in fractional laser technology. Operating at 1,440 nanometers with low fluence parameters, this system is specifically designed for early intervention in photoaging and maintenance of skin health. The treatment creates very shallow thermal injury in the superficial dermis, making it ideal for patients seeking minimal downtime while addressing early signs of aging.

The Clear+Brilliant system has become popular for younger patients and those pursuing preventative dermatology. It requires minimal recovery time and can be incorporated into regular skincare maintenance regimens.

Mechanism of Action

Clear+Brilliant operates through fractional photothermolysis at the dermoepidermal junction level. The 1,440-nanometer wavelength creates very shallow zones of thermal coagulation, triggering mild inflammatory response and collagen remodeling without ablating tissue. The fractional approach ensures rapid healing as the surrounding untreated skin facilitates re-epithelialization.

Benefits of this gentle approach include:

  • Virtually no downtime required
  • Minimal discomfort during treatment
  • Suitable for all skin types
  • Can be repeated frequently for maintenance
  • Ideal for early photoaging and prevention
  • Compatible with other skincare treatments

Clinical Applications

Clear+Brilliant is indicated for early photoaging, fine surface texture irregularities, and as a preventative measure for younger patients. The procedure improves skin tone and clarity while stimulating low-level collagen remodeling. Many dermatologists recommend Clear+Brilliant for patients 25-35 years old as a preventative measure against progressive photoaging.

Treatment Experience

Clear+Brilliant treatments are performed with topical anesthesia or without anesthesia due to minimal discomfort. The procedure typically takes 15-30 minutes for facial treatment. Patients experience mild warmth and slight snapping sensation during treatment. No cooling or special post-operative care is required beyond standard sunscreen use.

Results and Expectations

Results from Clear+Brilliant are subtle and cumulative. Most patients notice improved skin tone and texture clarity within 1-2 weeks of treatment. Optimal results develop over 8-12 weeks as collagen remodeling occurs. The procedure is typically repeated every 2-4 weeks for 4-6 sessions, with maintenance treatments every 2-3 months thereafter.

While Clear+Brilliant provides more modest results than more aggressive fractional lasers, the minimal downtime and preventative benefits make it attractive for maintenance-focused patients. Combined treatments with other modalities are common.

Safety and Tolerability

Clear+Brilliant demonstrates excellent safety across all Fitzpatrick skin types. Adverse effects are minimal, with occasional temporary erythema and mild edema. The gentle nature of the treatment makes it suitable for fragile periorbital skin and all facial areas.
