Clear+Brilliant Laser: What Every Patient Should Know

By DermoBrain Medical Editorial Team Updated 2026-03-06 Medically Reviewed ⏱ 2 min read

Clear+Brilliant (Solta Medical) at 1,540 nm represents an entry-level non-ablative fractional laser for early photoaging prevention and maintenance. Creating microscopic thermal zones with virtually no downtime, Clear+Brilliant is ideal for patients seeking preventative treatments or early intervention.

Technology and Specifications

Clear+Brilliant uses 1,540 nm erbium-doped fiber laser in fractionated delivery mode. Microthermal zone density varies from low to high treatment intensity. Standard settings treat 15-25% of surface per pass. Pulse durations and fluence are automated, making it accessible and reproducible across practitioners.

Clinical Applications

Ideal for prevention of photoaging, early fine lines, mild pigmentation irregularities, and general skin rejuvenation maintenance. Safe for all Fitzpatrick skin types including darker skin. Particularly effective for patients in 20s-40s seeking preventative treatments or early intervention.

Treatment Protocol

Quick office treatments requiring only topical anesthesia. Full-face treatment 15-20 minutes. Patients experience minimal discomfort. Recommended treatment series: 4-6 sessions monthly for optimal results. Can be repeated every 3-6 months for maintenance.

Recovery and Downtime

Virtually no downtime—main defining feature. Minimal erythema resolves within hours. No crusting or peeling. Patients can apply makeup immediately. Return to work and normal activities same day. Makes it ideal for busy professionals.

Results and Expectations

Improvement in skin texture and fine lines becomes apparent after 2-3 treatments. 30-50% improvement in fine lines after complete treatment series. Results most evident for early photoaging. Cumulative improvement with maintenance treatments. Results less dramatic than Fraxel but dramatically more convenient.

Safety Profile

Excellent safety record. Temporary mild erythema (few hours). Transient hyperpigmentation 5-10%, resolves within 1-2 weeks. No reported cases of permanent hypopigmentation or scarring. Suitable for all skin types including highly photosensitive patients.

Conclusion

Clear+Brilliant at 1,540 nm provides convenient preventative rejuvenation with virtually no downtime. Ideal entry point for patients beginning aesthetic treatments. Progressive improvement with minimal risk makes it excellent for early photoaging and maintenance.
