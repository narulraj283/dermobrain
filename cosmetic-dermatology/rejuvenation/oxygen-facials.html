Oxygen Facials: Pressurized Oxygen and Topical Serums

Oxygen facials utilize pressurized medical-grade oxygen to propel specialized serums containing vitamins, peptides, hyaluronic acid, and other beneficial ingredients into the skin. This non-invasive treatment improves skin texture, tone, hydration, and radiance while promoting cellular regeneration and collagen production through enhanced oxygen delivery and serum absorption.

Mechanism of Action

Pressurized oxygen disrupts the superficial epidermis, allowing deeper penetration of topical serums while simultaneously delivering oxygen molecules that enhance cellular metabolism and ATP production. The oxygen-stimulated fibroblast activity increases collagen and elastin synthesis, promoting skin firmness and elasticity. Enhanced cellular oxygenation improves skin appearance and accelerates healing processes.

Serum Formulations

Oxygen facials employ specialized serum formulations containing ingredients such as hyaluronic acid for hydration, vitamin C for antioxidant protection and brightening, peptides for collagen stimulation, niacinamide for oil control, and various botanical extracts for anti-inflammatory and soothing effects. Custom serums can be selected based on individual skin concerns and types.

Clinical Applications

Oxygen facials effectively address fine lines and wrinkles, loss of radiance, dull complexion, uneven tone and texture, enlarged pores, and general skin quality improvement. The treatment is particularly beneficial for individuals with sensitive skin or those unable to tolerate more aggressive procedures. Results are cumulative with regular treatments.

Treatment Protocol and Duration

Typical oxygen facial treatments last 30-60 minutes and can be performed weekly or bi-weekly for optimal cumulative effects. The procedure is completely painless and produces no downtime, allowing immediate return to normal activities and makeup application. Regular treatments maintain improved skin appearance and collagen production over time.

Safety and Suitability

Oxygen facials are suitable for all skin types including sensitive, reactive, and compromised skin. The treatment carries minimal risk of irritation or adverse effects. Individuals with active herpes simplex or severe acne may benefit from modifications or alternative timing. The gentle nature of the treatment makes it ideal for those with limited downtime tolerance.
