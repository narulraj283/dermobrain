Understanding Onychomycosis: Key Information

Proper understanding of Onychomycosis is essential for appropriate clinical management and patient outcomes.

Overview

First-line therapies for Onychomycosis have established efficacy and favorable safety profiles.

  • Terbinafine: 250 mg daily for 6-12 weeks (highest cure rates for dermatophytes)
  • Itraconazole: 200 mg daily for 12 weeks or pulsed dosing (400 mg for one week monthly)
  • Fluconazole: 150-300 mg weekly for 4-6 months
