Dupilumab: Evidence-Based Clinical Management

By DermoBrain Medical Editorial Team Updated 2026-03-03 Medically Reviewed

Dupilumab (Dupixent) represents the first FDA-approved biologic for atopic dermatitis, targeting IL-4 receptor-alpha to block both IL-4 and IL-13 signaling. Clinical trials demonstrated efficacy in moderate-to-severe AD with approximately 50-75% of patients achieving 75% improvement in disease activity (EASI-75).
