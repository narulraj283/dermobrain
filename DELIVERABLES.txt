CONTENT SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Patient Title:
  - Length: Variable (simplified from clinical)
  - Format: Plain English, no medical jargon
  - Examples:
    * "Melanoma, Staging, and Treatment"
    * "Merkel Cell Carcinoma"
    * "Post-Inflammatory Hyperpigmentation in Dark Skin"

Patient Content:
  - Length: 1,015-1,099 words
  - Format: HTML with semantic markup
  - Reading Level: Grade 8-10
  - Structure:
    * H1: Article title
    * Bottom Line summary (styled box)
    * H2: "What You Need to Know"
    * H2: "Common Questions about [condition]"
    * H3: 3-4 FAQ questions
    * H2: "Taking Care of Your Skin"
    * H2: "When Should You See a Dermatologist"
    * H2: "References"
  - References: 8 real citations per article
  - Style: Professional, accessible, reassuring

Patient Meta Description:
  - Length: 104-155 characters
  - Format: SEO optimized for Google
  - Style: Patient-focused, outcome-oriented
  - Example: "Understanding melanoma, staging, and treatment. Learn what you need to know, treatment options, and how to protect your skin."

Patient Tags:
  - Count: 5-8 tags
  - Format: Plain English, lowercase
  - Style: Patient-friendly keywords
  - Examples: "melanoma", "skin cancer", "treatment options", "sun protection"
