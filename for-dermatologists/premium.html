Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get approved for premium listing? ▼

Our verification process typically takes 3-5 business days. We'll review your credentials and practice information to ensure accuracy. Once approved, your premium listing goes live immediately with the gold badge and enhanced features.

Can I manage my appointments through DermoBrain? ▼

Yes! Premium listings include our integrated appointment booking system. Patients can view your availability and book appointments directly through your listing. The system integrates with most major practice management software, and appointments sync automatically.

What kind of analytics will I receive? ▼

Premium members receive a comprehensive monthly analytics report including: profile views, appointment booking inquiries, search keywords that bring patients to you, geographic patient distribution, and comparison metrics with similar practices in your area.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract? ▼

No setup fees and no long-term contracts required! We offer flexible month-to-month billing so you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Most practices see positive ROI within the first month of their premium listing.

How do premium listings improve my visibility? ▼

Premium listings appear at the top of search results for your specialties and location. They also get featured in DermoBrain articles, receive the gold badge verification mark for increased trust, and are included in specialty-specific directories that free listings don't appear in.
