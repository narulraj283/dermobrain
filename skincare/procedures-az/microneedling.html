Microneedling Overview

First-line therapies for Microneedling have established efficacy and favorable safety profiles.

  • Atrophic scars (ice pick, boxcar, and rolling acne scars)
  • Traumatic scars
  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Loss of skin elasticity and laxity
  • Uneven skin texture and roughness
  • Stretch marks
  • Alopecia (hair loss)
  • Hyperpigmentation and discoloration
